What is CSS Subgrid?

Design mockups often show perfectly aligned layouts, but real-world content varies—like one testimonial being a short sentence and another a full paragraph—making alignment tricky. CSS Subgridsolves this by aligning nested elements with their parent grid, ensuring a consistent look despite content differences.
